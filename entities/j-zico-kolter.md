# J. Zico Kolter

**소속:** Carnegie Mellon University, School of Computer Science & Bosch Center for AI

## 주요 연구 방향

- Differentiable optimization layers (OptNet, NeurIPS 2017)
- Implicit-depth models (DEQ + monDEQ, 합동 저자 Bai & Winston)
- Adversarial robustness and certified defenses
- Monotone operator theory 기반 신경망

## 주요 논문

- **monDEQ (NeurIPS 2020)** — Monotone Operator Equilibrium Networks (Winston & Kolter). Guaranteed stable convergence via monotone operator splitting.
- **DEQ (NeurIPS 2019)** — Deep Equilibrium Models (Bai, Kolter, Koltun). 최초의 실용적 equilibrium network.
- **OptNet (ICML 2017)** — Differentiable optimization as a layer (Amos & Kolter).
