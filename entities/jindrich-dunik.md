# Jindřich Duník

Professor at University of West Bohemia (UWB), Department of Cybernetics. Expert in **nonlinear filtering, stochastic systems, and Bayesian estimation**.

## 주요 기여

- **UKF scaling parameter adaptive setting** (with [[miroslav-simandl|Miroslav Šimandl]] & [[ondrej-straka|Ondřej Straka]], IEEE TAC 2012)
- **Derivative-free filters** — Stochastic integration filter, quadrature-based Kalman filter
- **Point-mass filter (PMF)** — Adaptive grid filtering for non-Gaussian systems
- **Stochastic integration filter (SIF)** — Novel quadrature rule for Gaussian filtering
