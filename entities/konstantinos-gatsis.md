# Konstantinos Gatsis

**Konstantinos Gatsis** is a researcher at the **University of Oxford, Department of Engineering Science**, specializing in control theory, state estimation, and learning-based dynamical systems.

## Key Contributions

- **Miao Robust Observer** (2023): Co-authored with [[keyan-miao]] the Neural ODE-based robust state observer framework, analyzing the convergence-robustness trade-off in [[buisson-fenet-kkl-observer|KKL observers]].

## Research Interests

- Nonlinear state estimation and observer design
- Neural ODEs for dynamical systems
- Robust control and learning-based control
- Optimal control theory

## Selected Publications

- Miao, K., & Gatsis, K. (2023). Learning Robust State Observers using Neural ODEs. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2212.00866*.
