# Miroslav Šimandl

Professor at University of West Bohemia (UWB). Long-time leader in **stochastic systems, state estimation, and nonlinear filtering** research. Head of the UWB filtering group.

## 주요 기여

- **Adaptive UKF scaling parameter setting** (with [[jindrich-dunik|Jindřich Duník]], IEEE TAC 2012)
- **Stochastic integration filter** — novel Gaussian filtering framework
- **Point-mass filter (PMF)** — Global filtering for non-Gaussian distributions
- **EM-based parameter identification** in stochastic systems
