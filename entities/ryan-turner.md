# Ryan Turner

Research scientist formerly at Uber AI and DeepMind. Research focuses on **Gaussian processes**, Bayesian optimization, uncertainty quantification, and machine learning for state-space models.

## 연구 분야
- Gaussian processes for state-space models
- Bayesian optimization (local optimization, scalable BO)
- Uncertainty quantification in machine learning
- Change point detection
- Hyperparameter optimization

## 주요 기여
- **UKF-L** — Model-based learning of sigma points in Unscented Kalman Filtering (with [[carl-edward-rasmussen]], 2010)
- **GP-ADF** — Gaussian Process Approximate Assumed Density Filtering
- **Scalable Bayesian optimization** — Local optimization strategies for high-dimensional BO
- **Gaussian process change point detection** — GP models for detecting regime changes in time series
