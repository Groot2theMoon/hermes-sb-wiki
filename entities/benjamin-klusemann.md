# Benjamin Klusemann

Benjamin Klusemann is the senior/corresponding author of *"Generative reconstruction of 2D and 3D polycrystalline microstructures using symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics"* (arXiv:2605.14898, 2026). His research group developed a differentiable microstructure characterization and reconstruction framework based on symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics (SHSH) for the generative synthesis of 3D polycrystalline volumes from 2D EBSD data.

## 주요 기여

### Generative Polycrystalline Microstructure Reconstruction (2026)
- Led the development of the **SHSH-based orientation representation** and differentiable reconstruction framework implemented in MCRpy
- Oversaw the demonstration of **3D microstructure recovery from 2D orientation data** of aluminum alloys after thermo-mechanical processing, showing successful recovery of morphological features and crystallographic distributions
