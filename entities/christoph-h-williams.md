# Christopher K. I. Williams

Professor of Machine Learning at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. Known for foundational contributions to **Gaussian processes**, kernel methods, and Bayesian machine learning. Co-author of the seminal textbook *Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning* (MIT Press, 2006) with Carl Edward Rasmussen. Fellow of the Royal Society of Edinburgh (FRSE, 2021).

## 연구 분야
- Gaussian processes and kernel methods
- Bayesian machine learning
- Computer vision and object recognition
- Time-series modeling and dynamical systems
- Multi-task learning

## 주요 기여
- **Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning** (2006, MIT Press) — the standard textbook, co-authored with [[carl-edward-rasmussen]] (DeGroot Prize 2009)
- **PASCAL VOC** — Co-organizer of the Visual Object Classes challenge (2005–2012)
- **GP state-space models** — Gaussian process models for dynamical systems
- **Multi-task dynamical systems** — Sharing statistical strength across related time series
- **Early work on GP classification** — Probabilistic approaches to kernel-based classification
