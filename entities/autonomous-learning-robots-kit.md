# Autonomous Learning Robots (ALR) Lab @ KIT

Autonomous Learning Robots Lab, Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany. PI: [[gerhard-neumann]].

## 주요 연구 방향
- Probabilistic machine learning for robotics
- Recurrent Kalman Networks (RKN) 및 변형 (ac-RKN, 등)
- Model-based reinforcement learning (PILCO 계열)
- Robot dynamics learning (hydraulic, pneumatic, soft robots)
- Learning from demonstration, inverse reinforcement learning
