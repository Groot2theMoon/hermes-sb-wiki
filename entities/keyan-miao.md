# Keyan Miao

**Keyan Miao** is a researcher at the **University of Oxford, Department of Engineering Science**. Their primary research focuses on learning-based state observers for nonlinear dynamical systems using [[neural-odes|Neural ODEs]].

## Key Contributions

- **Miao Robust Observer** (2023): Proposed a Neural ODE-based framework for learning Luenberger and [[buisson-fenet-kkl-observer|KKL observers]], with explicit analysis of the **convergence speed vs. robustness trade-off** in KKL observer eigenvalues. Demonstrated that training with noise and eigenvalue regularization improves observer robustness.

## Selected Publications

- Miao, K., & Gatsis, K. (2023). Learning Robust State Observers using Neural ODEs. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2212.00866*.
