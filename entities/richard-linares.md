# Richard Linares

Director's Postdoctoral Fellow at **Los Alamos National Laboratory** (LANL), Intelligence and Space Research division. Expert in advanced filtering for astrodynamics and space situational awareness.

## 주요 기여

- **Generalized Gaussian Cubature (GGC)** — 임의 정확도 차수의 multidimensional cubature for Gaussian filters (with [[john-crassidis|John Crassidis]], 2015, AAS)
- Nonlinear filtering for space object tracking and orbit determination
- Machine learning for cislunar space domain awareness
