# Hashim A. Hashim

Professor at **Carleton University** (Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering), Ottawa, Canada. Expert in **navigation systems, sensor fusion, nonlinear state estimation, and robotics**.

## 주요 기여

- **DeepUKF-VIN** (with [[khashayar-ghanizadegan|Khashayar Ghanizadegan]], 2025) — Quaternion-based deep adaptive UKF for 3D visual-inertial navigation
- Deep learning-based adaptation for Kalman-type filters
- GPS-denied navigation, UAV pose estimation
- Sensor fusion: IMU + vision + UWB
