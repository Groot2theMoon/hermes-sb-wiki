# Shenglun Yi

Researcher at **University of Padova** (Dipartimento di Ingegneria dell'Informazione), working on **robust state estimation, Kalman filtering under model uncertainty, and nonlinear filtering**.

## 주요 기여

- **Robust sigma point Kalman filtering** (with [[mattia-zorzi|Mattia Zorzi]], 2025) — Dynamic minimax game + sigma point approximation for nonlinear robust estimation
- **Robust Kalman filtering under model uncertainty** (with Zorzi, *IEEE TAC* 2021) — Degenerate densities case
- **Update-resilient Kalman filtering** (with Zorzi, 2024)
- **Robust EKF for tuned liquid damper dynamics** (2021 ACC)
