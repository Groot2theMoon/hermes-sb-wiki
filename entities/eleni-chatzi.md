# Eleni Chatzi

**Professor** at the Department of Civil, Environmental and Geomatic Engineering, ETH Zürich. Chair of the Institute of Structural Engineering (IBK). Affiliated with the Singapore-ETH Centre, Future Resilient Systems program.

## Research Areas

- [[neural-odes|Neural ODEs]] for structural dynamics
- Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)
- Interpretable machine learning for nonlinear dynamics
- System identification and virtual sensing
- Data-driven structural engineering

## Key Contributions

- **PiGGO** ([[pigg-graph-kalman-filter]]): Physics-Guided Graph Kalman Filter integrating GNODE with EKF for virtual sensing of nonlinear structures under uncertainty
- Co-author of **SKANODE** ([[skanode]]), introducing structured Kolmogorov-Arnold Neural ODEs for interpretable symbolic discovery of nonlinear dynamics from partial observations
- Extensive work on physics-encoded deep learning for dynamical systems
- Research on structural monitoring, vibration analysis, and digital twins for infrastructure

## Affiliations

- **ETH Zürich** — Department of Civil, Environmental and Geomatic Engineering
- **Singapore-ETH Centre** — Future Resilient Systems (FRS)
- Editor/Editorial board member for several structural engineering journals
