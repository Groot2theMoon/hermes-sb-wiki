# Mona Buisson-Fenet

**Researcher** at Mines Paris - PSL University (Centre Automatique et Systèmes) and Ansys France (Ansys Research Team), with affiliation at the Institute for Data Science in Mechanical Engineering, RWTH Aachen University.

## Research Areas

- [[neural-odes|Neural ODEs]] for nonlinear system identification
- KKL observer theory and nonlinear observer design
- [[state-space-model|State-space model]] learning from partial observations
- Physics-aware machine learning for dynamical systems
- Joint state and dynamics estimation

## Key Contributions

- Author of **Recognition Models to Learn Dynamics from Partial Observations with Neural ODEs** (TMLR 2023), introducing [[buisson-fenet-kkl-observer|KKL observer-based recognition models]] for NODEs — the first work to connect nonlinear observer theory with Neural ODE training under partial observability
- Research on high-gain observers with Gaussian process models for joint state and dynamics estimation (IEEE L-CSS, 2021)
- Work on numerical gain tuning for KKL observers (2022)
