# Andrew Gracyk

**Andrew Gracyk** is a researcher affiliated with the Department of Mathematics at Purdue University. His work lies at the intersection of control theory, ordinary differential equations, and machine learning, with a focus on observability conditions for neural state-space models and the Mamba architecture.

## Affiliations

- **Department of Mathematics**, Purdue University
- Previously: University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), Department of Statistics

## Research Areas

- Control theory for neural state-space models
- Observability analysis of dynamical systems
- Mamba architecture and structured state-space models (S4, S6)
- Neural ordinary differential equations
- Machine learning theory

## Selected Publications

- Gracyk, A. (2025). Observability conditions for neural state-space models with eigenvalues and their roots of unity. arXiv:2504.15758. [[observability-nssm]]
