# Rebecca Willett

**Rebecca Willett** is a professor in the Department of Statistics and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Chicago. She is also affiliated with the NSF-Simons National Institute for Theory and Mathematics in Biology (NITMB). Her research focuses on machine learning for inverse problems, data assimilation, high-dimensional statistics, and imaging.

## Affiliations

- **Professor**, Department of Statistics, University of Chicago
- **Professor**, Department of Computer Science, University of Chicago
- **Affiliate**, NSF-Simons National Institute for Theory and Mathematics in Biology
- Previously: Professor at University of Wisconsin-Madison (Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering)

## Research Areas

- Data assimilation and state estimation
- Machine learning for inverse problems and imaging
- High-dimensional statistics and optimization
- Computational optimal transport
- Scientific machine learning
