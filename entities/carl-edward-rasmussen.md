# Carl Edward Rasmussen

Professor at University of Cambridge, Department of Engineering. Known for foundational contributions to **Gaussian Processes** and **machine learning for dynamical systems**.

## 주요 기여

- **Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning** (2006, MIT Press) — the standard textbook, with [[christoph-h-williams|Christopher K. I. Williams]]
- **UKF-L** — Model-based learning of sigma points in Unscented Kalman Filtering (with [[ryan-turner|Ryan Turner]], 2010)
- **GP-ADF** — Gaussian Process Approximate Assumed Density Filtering
- **Gaussian Process state-space models** — Learning nonparametric dynamical systems via GPs
