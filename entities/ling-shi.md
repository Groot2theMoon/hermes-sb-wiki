# Ling Shi

Ling Shi is a professor at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), specializing in Kalman filtering, networked control systems, sensor scheduling, and secure state estimation. He has made influential contributions to estimation and control over communication networks.

## 주요 기여

### Variational Robust Kalman Filters: A Unified Framework (2026)
- Co-developed a unified variational robust Kalman filter framework that integrates **robustness and adaptivity** under complex noise environments
- Contributed to bridging variational Bayesian methods with **Student's t-distribution** based robust filtering, enabling superior performance under both outliers and time-varying noise
- The framework supports seamless recovery of standard KF, robust KF, and adaptive KF through parameter tuning
- RIGOR 관련성: Extends robust KF theory to networked and cyber-physical settings — central to RIGOR's focus on reliable filtering under adverse conditions
