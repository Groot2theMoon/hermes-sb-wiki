# Ping Lin (林平)

Ping Lin is a researcher at the College of Automobile and Traffic Engineering, Nanjing Forestry University, Nanjing, China. He contributed to the application of modified particle swarm optimization for identifying non-acoustical parameters of porous materials.

## 주요 기여 (Key Contributions)

### MPSO 기반 JCA 파라미터 식별 (Xu & Lin 2021)
- *PLOS ONE* 16(5), e0250950 게재 (38회 인용)
- Modified Particle Swarm Optimization (MPSO) 알고리즘을 Jute fiber felt의 JCA 비음향 파라미터 식별에 공동 적용
- 실험 검증(impedance tube SAC 측정) 및 결과 분석 공동 수행
- Writing – original draft, Writing – review & editing, Formal analysis 담당
