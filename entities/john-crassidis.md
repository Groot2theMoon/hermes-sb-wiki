# John L. Crassidis

CUBRC Professor in Space Situational Awareness at University at Buffalo (SUNY). Leading expert in **astrodynamics, spacecraft attitude estimation, and nonlinear filtering**.

## 주요 기여

- **Generalized Gaussian Cubature** for nonlinear filtering (with [[richard-linares|Richard Linares]], 2015, AAS)
- **Unscented filtering for spacecraft attitude estimation** — widely cited standard reference
- 저서: "Optimal Estimation of Dynamic Systems" (with John L. Junkins, CRC Press)
- Sigma-point filtering for orbit determination, attitude, and space situational awareness
