# Youssef M. Marzouk

Professor at **Massachusetts Institute of Technology** (Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems). Director of the MIT Uncertainty Quantification group. Leading expert in **Bayesian inverse problems, optimal transport for inference, measure transport, and uncertainty quantification**.

## 주요 기여

- **Exact affine conditioning beyond Gaussians** (with [[frederic-jorgensen|Frederic Jorgensen]], 2025) — Unique characterization of the ensemble Kalman update
- **Transport map methods for Bayesian inference** — Triangular transport maps, conditional density estimation
- **Optimal transport for data assimilation**
- **Measure transport for inverse problems**
- **Polynomial chaos and spectral methods for UQ**
