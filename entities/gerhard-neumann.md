# Gerhard Neumann

Professor at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), head of the **Autonomous Learning Robots (ALR)** lab. Research focuses on robot learning, model-based reinforcement learning, optimal transport, deep dynamics models, and human-robot interaction. Recipient of ERC Consolidator Grant (SMARTI³ project).

## 연구 분야
- Robot learning and manipulation
- Model-based reinforcement learning (PILCO 계열)
- Recurrent Kalman Networks (RKN) and deep dynamics models
- Optimal transport for robotics
- Imitation learning and learning from demonstration
- Human-robot interaction and intuitive robot teaching

## 주요 기여
- **ALR Lab @ KIT** — Founded and leads the Autonomous Learning Robots laboratory
- **Recurrent Kalman Networks (RKN)** — Supervision of RKN/ac-RKN research for probabilistic state estimation in robotics
- **ERC Consolidator Grant (SMARTI³)** — Scalable Manipulation Learning through AR-Enhanced Teleoperation
- **Probabilistic movement primitives (ProMPs)** — Coordination of multi-human-robot tasks
- **Model-based RL** — Data-efficient policy learning for complex robot domains
