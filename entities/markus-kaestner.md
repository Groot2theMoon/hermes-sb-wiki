# Markus Kästner (Markus Kaestner)

**Markus Kästner** (also romanized as Markus Kaestner) is a co-author of *"Generative reconstruction of 2D and 3D polycrystalline microstructures using symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics"* (arXiv:2605.14898, 2026). He contributed to the development of a differentiable microstructure characterization and reconstruction framework using symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics (SHSH) for polycrystalline materials.

## 주요 기여

### Polycrystalline Microstructure Reconstruction (2026)
- Co-developed the **SHSH-based orientation representation** and differentiable reconstruction framework in MCRpy
- Contributed to the recovery of 3D polycrystalline volumes from 2D EBSD cross-sections of aluminum alloys
