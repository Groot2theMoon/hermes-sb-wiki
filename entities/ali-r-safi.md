# Ali R. Safi

Ali R. Safi is the first author of *"Generative reconstruction of 2D and 3D polycrystalline microstructures using symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics"* (arXiv:2605.14898, 2026), which introduces a symmetrized hyperspherical harmonics (SHSH) representation for differentiable microstructure characterization and reconstruction of polycrystalline materials.

## 주요 기여

### Generative Polycrystalline Microstructure Reconstruction (2026)
- Proposed a **differentiable SHSH-based orientation representation** to overcome numerical singularities of Euler-angle methods for polycrystalline microstructures
- Developed a **cGAN and gradient-based reconstruction framework** in MCRpy for 3D volume synthesis from 2D EBSD data
- Demonstrated recovery of morphological features and crystallographic distribution for aluminum alloys after thermo-mechanical processing
