# Frederic J. N. Jorgensen

PhD student / Researcher at **Massachusetts Institute of Technology** (Department of Mathematics). Works on **stochastic filtering, measure transport, ensemble methods, and uncertainty quantification**.

## 주요 기여

- **Exact affine conditioning beyond Gaussians** (with [[youssef-marzouk|Youssef M. Marzouk]], 2025) — Complete characterization of the ensemble Kalman update's exact set and its uniqueness
- Analysis of distributional fixed-point equations for affine transport maps
- Theoretical foundations of ensemble Kalman filter in non-Gaussian regimes
