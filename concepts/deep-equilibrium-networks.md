# Deep Equilibrium Networks (DEQ)

DEQ models implicitly define the output of a deep network as the fixed point of a layer function, eliminating the need for explicit stacking of layers. Related to [[monotone-operator-equilibrium-networks|monDEQ]] which enforces monotone operator constraints for guaranteed convergence.
