# Mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of physics concerned with the motion of bodies and the forces acting upon them, forming the theoretical foundation for solid mechanics, fluid dynamics, and structural analysis. It serves as the overarching domain for physics-informed machine learning and neural operator methods applied to physical systems.

## 개요

Mechanics is traditionally divided into statics (equilibrium of stationary bodies), kinematics (description of motion without forces), and dynamics (motion under forces). Its governing equations — Newton's laws, conservation of momentum, angular momentum, and energy — underpin all engineering disciplines. Within the wiki, mechanics provides the unifying umbrella under which specific sub-domains (solid mechanics, fluid dynamics, structural analysis, acoustics) are organized, with physics-informed ML methods providing a unified computational framework across these sub-fields.
