# Acoustics

Acoustics is the branch of physics concerned with the generation, propagation, and reception of mechanical waves in solids, fluids, and gases. In the context of physics-informed machine learning and neural operators, acoustics serves as a key application domain for surrogate modeling of wave propagation, scattering, and absorption phenomena.

## 개요

Acoustics encompasses a wide range of phenomena from audible sound to ultrasonics and infrasound, governed fundamentally by the wave equation and its variants such as the Helmholtz equation in the frequency domain. Engineering applications include noise control, architectural acoustics, acoustic metamaterial design, and non-destructive evaluation. The integration of ML with acoustics — particularly physics-informed neural operators (PINOs) and DeepONets for Helmholtz problems — has emerged as a major research direction for accelerating simulation-driven design of acoustic materials and structures.
