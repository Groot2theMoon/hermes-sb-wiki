# Structural Analysis

Structural analysis is the discipline concerned with predicting the behavior of structures under mechanical, thermal, and environmental loads, including deformations, internal forces, stresses, and stability. It is a core application domain for physics-informed surrogate models, digital twins, and data-driven filtering methods for health monitoring.

## 개요

Structural analysis spans static and dynamic regimes, linear and nonlinear material behavior, and a wide range of structural systems from bridges and buildings to aerospace frames and MEMS devices. Key methods include finite element analysis (FEA), modal analysis, and nonlinear time-history analysis. Recent advances in physics-informed ML, Koopman-based digital twins, and neural Kalman filtering have enabled real-time structural health monitoring, surrogate modeling for rapid simulation, and data-driven damage detection under uncertainty.
