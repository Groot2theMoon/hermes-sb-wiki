# Micromechanics and Homogenization

The study of how micro-scale material properties determine macro-scale effective behavior. Homogenization bridges scales by computing effective properties from periodic representative volume elements (RVEs). See also [[deep-material-network|DMN]] and [[fft-homogenization-composites]].
