# Solid Mechanics

Solid mechanics is the branch of mechanics concerned with the behavior of solid materials under external loads, including deformation, stress distribution, and failure. It is foundational to structural engineering, materials science, and geomechanics, and is a primary domain for physics-informed neural operators, constitutive modeling, and surrogate-based design.

## 개요

Solid mechanics encompasses linear elasticity, plasticity, viscoelasticity, fracture mechanics, and fatigue, governed by equilibrium equations, constitutive laws, and compatibility conditions. The field has seen rapid integration with machine learning methods — including Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), Deep Material Networks (DMNs), and physics-encoded neural operators (PeFNO) — for tasks such as stress field prediction, homogenization, constitutive model discovery, and inverse design of mechanical metamaterials.
