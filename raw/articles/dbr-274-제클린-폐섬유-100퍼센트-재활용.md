## Comprehensive Summary

### Overview
- **Company:** 제클린 (Zeklin) – a social enterprise based in Jeju, founded in 2017 by CEO Cha Seung-su (차승수, age 53).
- **Mission:** Collect textile waste (towels, bedding, work uniforms) from hotels, resorts, and companies; recycle it into new yarn, fabric, and finished products (towels, t-shirts, bedding).
- **Brands:**
  - **RE;FeaT (리피트):** Recycled raw material, yarn, and fabric brand (launched 2022).
  - **2bd (투비디):** Finished product brand (towels, t-shirts, bedding) made from RE;FeaT fabric (launched 2024).

### Business Model – Closed-Loop Textile Recycling
1. **Collection:** Collects textile waste from partner accommodations (74 current partners, total 281 served since 2017).
2. **Shredding & Fiberization:** Waste is shredded into cotton fluff.
3. **Spinning:** Fluff is spun into white recycled yarn – white allows easy dyeing/pattern application.
4. **Fabric & Product Production:** Yarn becomes fabric, then final products (towels, apparel, bedding).
5. **Re‑collection & Re‑shredding:** Used products can be collected again and re‑entered into the cycle.

### Key Achievements (as of article date)
- **Total textile waste recycled into RE;FeaT fabric:** ~18.6 tons (since 2022).
- **Total recycled yarn produced:** ~6.7 tons (since 2022).
- **100% recycling rate:** Now able to recycle all waste except buttons, zippers, and other trims (improved from 60% previously).

### Partnerships & ESG Collaborations
- ESG joint agreements with major Jeju businesses:
  - **Maison Glad Jeju**
  - **Lotte Hotels & Resorts**
  - **Jeju Shinhwa World**
- Partners provide waste and also purchase recycled products.

### Support & Growth
- CEO credits **SE Consultant** program (matches SK Group alumni mentors with social venture CEOs) for helping with market analysis, HR, and financial advice in early stages.
- The program provided systematic consulting that clarified market structure and business growth strategies.

### Future Vision
- CEO aims to make recycled textiles a daily norm: "10년 후 사람들이 새 옷, 헌 옷 구분 없이 재생 섬유로 만든 의류를 일상적으로 구매하고 입는 시대" (In 10 years, people will routinely buy and wear clothes made from recycled fibers without distinguishing between new and old).
- Actively gathering related startups to push for legislation on textile waste and circular recycling.
