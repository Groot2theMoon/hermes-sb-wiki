# Toward AI-Driven Digital Twins for Metropolitan Floods: A Conditional Latent Dynamics Network Surrogate of the Shallow Water Equations

**Phillip Si, Yuan Qiu, Omar Sallam, Jeremy Feinstein, Ziang He, Eugene Yan, Peng Chen**

*arXiv:2605.13761v1 | Category: cs.LG*

## Abstract

AI-driven flood digital twins demand fast hydrodynamic surrogates for ensemble forecasting and observation assimilation. Yet even GPU-accelerated two-dimensional shallow water equation (SWE) solvers still require ~55 minutes per 96-hour run on a ~4.2-million-active-cell metropolitan basin, making such workloads prohibitive for real-time digital twin applications. We propose the Conditional Latent Dynamics Network (CLDNet), a neural PDE surrogate that learns the latent dynamics of the SWE in a compressed latent space via a conditional neural-ODE.
